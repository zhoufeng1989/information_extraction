Cracker Barrel Old Country Store Inc said it will post a fourth quarter charge of $9 million, or $0.15 per share, due to store closings.
Fourth quarter earnings per share, before the one-time, after-tax charge, will be in the range of $0.43 to $0.45, the company said.  
In the fourth quarter, comparable 13-week same store restaurant sales increased 1.9 percent and same store retail sales were up 7.3 percent, the company said.
With the closing of three stores, Cracker Barrel said it will have 257 stores. The company plans to open 50 new stores in fiscal 1997.
