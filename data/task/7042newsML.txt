National Steel Corp said on Tuesday its board of directors had named chairman Osamu Sawaragi chief executive.
He replaces V. John Goodwin, who resigned as chief executive, president and chief operating officer. Goodwin will remain as a consultant during the transition.
John Maczuzak, the vice president and general manager of the Granite City division, has been appointed executive vice president and acting chief operating officer.
National is the United States' fourth-largest integrated steel company. Its parent company is Japan's NKK Corp.
Sawaragi has been a director since 1990 and was elected chairman in January 1994.
