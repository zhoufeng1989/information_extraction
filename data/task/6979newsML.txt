-- Salomon Brothers said it downgraded Players International Inc to hold from buy.
-- The stock closed at 6-14/16 Tuesday.
-- Salomon cited "July jurisdictional results for both Illinois and Louisiana as well as margin pressure at the company's Lake Charles, La., and Metropolis, Ill., properties."  
-- Salomon's gaming analyst Bruce Turner also lowered his earnings estimates for the company, to $0.35 per share from $0.80 for 1996, to $0.45 from $1.15 for 1997, and to $0.55 from $1.25 for 1998.
-- The pared earnings outlook is based on "increased competitive pressures in the Lake Charles and Metropolis gaming markets."
