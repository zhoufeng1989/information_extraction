B.A.T Industries Plc said it expected to make a 50 million stg pre-tax profit from the sale by a subsidiary of Imasco Ltd of First Federal Savings and Loan Association for C$935 million.
Imasco is 41.5 percent-owned by B.A.T.  
HSBC Holdings' U.S. unit Marine Midland Bank bought First Federal Savings for more than C$935 million, comprising a sale price of C$843 million plus a pre-closing dividend from First Federal.
Imasco's 98 percent-owned CT Financial Services unit, that is selling First Federal Savings, will make a gain on the sale of about C$320 million, B.A.T said in a statement.
