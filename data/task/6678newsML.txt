Ascot Holding Plc's 290 million stg offer for Suter Plc was declared unconditional, after approval from a large majority of shareholders and warrant holders.
Ascot said in a statement it had received acceptances for shares representing 81.6 percent of the company.
Ascot made the offer, which valued each Suter share at about 237 pence, on July 25.
