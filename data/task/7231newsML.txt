Regional bank Advance Bank Ltd said on Wednesday it had reduced its range of fixed home loans to take advantage of money market expectations of the federal budget to secure lower cost funds.
The bank said it cut its one year fixed rate to 8.25 percent from 8.5 percent, two years to 8.5 percent from 8.95 percent and three year rate to 8.95percent from 9.25 percent.
In addition the bank said its four year fixed rate had been lowered to 9.25 percent from 9.75 percent and its five year rate to 9.45 percent from 9.75 percent.
