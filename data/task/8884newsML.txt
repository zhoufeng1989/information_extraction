Joseph E. Seagram & Sons Inc said on Thursday that it is merging its two United States based wine companies, Seagram Chateau & Estate Wines Co and Seagram Classics Wine Co.
Sam Bronfman II, currently president of The Seagram Classics Wine, will be president of the new entity and John Laird, presently president of Seagram Chateau & Estate, will be the new company's executive vice president.
Abdallah Simon, founder and chairman of Seagram Chateau & Estate, will be chairman of the new company.
Seagram said that the name of the new company, with revenues of over $200 million, will be announced later this fall.
