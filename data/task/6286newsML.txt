Keppel Integrated Engineering Ltd said on Wednesday its unit Keppel Engineering Pte Ltd, and Mitsui Engineering & Shipbuilding Co Ltd, had won orders to build overhead bridge cranes worth about Singapore $95 million.  
Keppel said in a statement the contracts involved the design, engineering, procurement, project management, construction, delivery, installation and commissioning of the cranes over a period of three years.
