Sportmart Inc said Thursday that Andrew Hochberg, president, had been named chief executive officer.
As CEO Hochberg succeeds his father Larry Hochberg, who remains chairman.
The company said Mark Scott was named president and elected to the board of directors. He joined Sportmart in July 1995 and had been an executive vice president.  
Sportmart said John Lowenstein was named to the newly created position of chief operating officer. Lowenstein had been executive vice president, operations.
Thomas Hendrickson has been promoted to executive vice president and continues as chief financial officer. He had been senior vice president and CFO since April 1995.
Sportmart operates 70 Sportmart sporting goods superstores, up from 59 at the end of the 1995 second quarter.
