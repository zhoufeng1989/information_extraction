Owens Corning said Wednesday it had appointed Steven Strobel as vice president and controller.
The company said Strobel will replace Domenico Cecere when he begins his new posting September 9.
It said Strobel was chief financial officer at the Canadian division of Kraft Foods.
Cecere became president of Owens Corning's Roofing and Asphalt business last December, it added.
