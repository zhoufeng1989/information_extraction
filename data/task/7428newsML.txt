Shares of Matsushita-Kotobuki Electronics Industries Ltd were higher by midday on Wednesday after the company raised its earnings forecast, brokers said.
At midday, the shares were up 160 yen at 2,760 with 277,000 shares traded.
On Tuesday, the firm revised its parent current profit forecast for the six months to September 30, 1996, to 9.80 billion yen from its previous outlook of 6.20 billion.
