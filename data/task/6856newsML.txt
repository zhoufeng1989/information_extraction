American HomePatient Inc said on Wednesday that it has acquired Flemington Medical Products Inc and Cranbury Medical Supplies Inc.
The terms of the acquisitions were not disclosed.
Both acquired companies provide respiratory services and home medical equipment and supplies. Combined, they add three new home health care centers and annualized revenues of $1.5 million, American HomePatient said.
Flemington and Cranbury were both owned by Roger Kreider who will remain with American HomePatient as general manager, the company said.
