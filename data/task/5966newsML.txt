Hungarian brokerage Citibank Befektetesi es Ertekpapir Kft named Istvan Farkas as managing director from September 1, Citibank said in a statement.
Farkas was deputy finance minister between 1989 and 1990 and a government administrator between 1994 and 1995.
Citibank brokerage, the wholly-owned Hungarian unit of Citibank, has been a member of the Budapest Stock Exchange since December 1995.
