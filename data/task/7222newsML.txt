Supermarket retailer Woolworths Ltd said the retail market generally remained subdued through July and August.  
"The retail market generally remains subdued through July and August," Woolworths said in a statement accompanying the 1995/96 results.
Woolworths earlier reported a slight increase in net profit to A$233.6 million in the 52 weeks to June 23, 1996.
Australia's largest grocer said it would continue its refurbishment programme and new store developments to gain incremental sales.
"These combined with initiatives to be announced will demonstrate the optimism the directors have for the future growth of the company," Woolworths said.
