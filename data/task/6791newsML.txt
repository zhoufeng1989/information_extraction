Fertiliser firm Egyptian Financial and Industrial(EFI) raised net profits for financial 1995/96 by 46.5 percent to 21.12 million pounds from 14.41 million a year earlier, company sources said on Wednesday.
But the firm, privatised by the state in May, kept dividends for the year ending June 30, 1995 steady at 3.6 pounds saying it would use most of the profits to fund a new production line.
EFI increased gross sales to 171.71 million for 1995/96 from 139.82 for financial 1994/95.
The firm's stock is one of the most active on the bourse and closed on Wednesday unchanged at 54.50 pounds.
