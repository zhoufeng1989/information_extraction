Druck Holdings said on Thursday it was proposing a capitalization issue of new shares in conjuction with its plans to transfer its shares to the London Stock Exchange's Official List from the Unlisted Securities Market.
Druck said 2.89 million stg would be capitalized from its reserves and applied in paying up in full, at par, 57,868,398 new "A' ordinary shares of 5p each in the company.
The shares will be issued to shareholders on the register at the close of business on 13 September 1996, Druck said.
