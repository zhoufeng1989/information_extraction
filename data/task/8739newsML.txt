Ross Systems Inc said it expects to see improved results in the first quarter of 1997, compared to a year ago, due to several deferred contracts which should be realized in the beginning of the year.
In the first quarter of fiscal 1996, Ross reported a loss of $0.25 a share on revenues of $14.2 million.
The company, which develops client/server business solutions, also said it expects its first quarter operating expenses to be below last year's expenses.
