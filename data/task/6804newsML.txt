Discount Investments and Koor Industries are the most popular stocks with Israeli mutual funds, a report by Meitav Investments and Securities said.
Bezeq Israel Telecom, I.D.B. Development Corp and Clal Electronics Industries are also in the top five.
Electra Consumer Products and Mofet-Israel Technology Fund are the two stocks which have the highest rate of investment from mutual funds, which hold 20 percent of their total shares each.
The report surveyed the portfolios of the nine biggest mutual fund managers.
