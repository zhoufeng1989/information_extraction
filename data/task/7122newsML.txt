Shares in Swedish insurer Trygg-Hansa AB made strong gains in the morning session after the company posted a 27 percent improvement in pre-tax profits to 1.52 billion crowns.
The share advanced 6.50 crowns to 119.50 crowns immediately after the publication of the results, but fell back to 117.0 by 1150 GMT.
Trygg-Hansa said in its six-month result statement that cost-cutting the the group had begun to pay off.
