Shares in shipping and transport group Koninklijke Nedlloyd rose nearly 10 percent or 3.40 guilders to 38.40 after the release of second quarter 1996 results and currently linger at 38.00 guilders per share.
Analysts had expected a small loss of about 10.8 million guilders for the second quarter.  
Nedlloyd announced a second quarter profit from ordinary operations of 21 million guilders and said it did not foresee a loss on ordinary operations in the full year of 1996.
"The market is looking more positively at Nedlloyd now," said one dealer, adding the price of the stock is likely to be boosted further on the upbeat news.
