St. Louis investor John Weil said Wednesday he holds a 5.21 percent stake in Allied Healthcare Products Inc or 405,300 common shares.
In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, Weil said he stake includes 105,200 common shares purchased between June 28 and August 20 at prices ranging from $6.50 and $8.625 per share.
Weil bought the shares for investment purposes, the filing said.
