Wolverine Tube Inc said on Wednesday it has agreed to acquire Tube Forming Inc and selected assets from two related entities for approximately $34 million.
"We expect it (Tube Forming) to be immediately accretive to our earnings, contributing from $0.22 to $0.25 per share for 1997," John Quarles, chairman, president and chief executive officer of Wolverine Tube, said in a statement.
The acquisition will be a cash transaction funded through the Wolverine's available cash and $75 million credit facility, the company said.  
Tube Forming Inc, a manufacturer of value-added copper fabricated products for the air conditioning and refrigeration industry, had sales in 1995 of approximately $24 million.
