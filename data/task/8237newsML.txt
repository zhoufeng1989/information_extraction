Fidelity Federal Bancorp said Thursday it planned to repurchase up to five percent of its outstanding common stock over the next 12 to 18 months.
"It is anticipated that the planned repurchase of the 125,000 shares would take place in the open market over the period if market conditions allow," M. Brian Davis, president, said in a statement.
Fidelity Federal is the holding company of United Fidelity Bank.
