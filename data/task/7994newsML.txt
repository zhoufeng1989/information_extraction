HSBC Markets has hired Paul Morganti to become debt origination director, it said in a statement on Thursday.
Morganti is currently executive director at Daiwa Europe, and will take up his new position on September 16, it added.
At HSBC Markets, Morganti will be responsible for developing capital markets business with sovereign and supranational clients, particularly in Europe and Canada, the statement said.
