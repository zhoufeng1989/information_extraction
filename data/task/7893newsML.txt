Didier-Werke AG, a German manufacturer of fireproof materials, said on Thursday it expected to a post a profit in 1996 at the group and parent level.
Earlier this month the company reported that first-half profit fell to 5.1 million marks from 11.1 million in the year-earlier period. First-half sales fell 5.5 percent to 637 million marks from 674 million marks.
