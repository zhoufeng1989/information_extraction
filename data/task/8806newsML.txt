Arcadian Corp said Thursday it had converted about 7.4 million convertible preferred shares into common stock.
Arcadian said it had changed the Series A shares into the same number of common shares on August 16.
The coversion will result in a net decrease of about $8 million in its annual dividends requirements, it said.
The company, which makes and markets nitrogen fertilizers and chemicals, said the conversion left about 38.5 million in common stock and 5.8 million preferred stock.
