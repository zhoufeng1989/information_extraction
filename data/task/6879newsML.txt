IDM Environmental Corp said Wednesday it expects to post higher 1996 revenues in the range of $60 million to $65 million.
The company said it also expects to sustain a pretax profit margin of eight percent to 12 percent.
The projected $60 million to $65 million in revenues for 1996 would represent an increase of between 50 percent and 65 percent from the previous year, the company said.
IDM is an environmental remediation company.
