OKOBANK and the co-operative banks will launch a 100 million markka variable rate 4.75 percent coupon bond to the public, OKOBANK said in a statement on Wednesday.
The maturity is three years, and interest is payable annually on August 27, it said.
The subscription period is August 27 to October 31, after which the bond will be listed on the Helsinki Stock Exchange.
