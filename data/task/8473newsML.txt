Austrian regional ultility company Energieversorgung Niederoesterreich (EVN) said on Thursday it was constructing a gas powerstation together with Hungarian firm Mol Rt in southwest Hungary.
Total investment is estimated at 1.2 billion schillings.  
Total plant power would be around 80 megawatts, EVN said in a statement, while annual production is expected at around 600 gigawatt hours.
The plant will be situated in Nagykanizsa. -- Vienna newsroom, +431 531 12 274
