Schuller Corp said on Thursday it had completed the acquisition of NRG Barriers Inc for about $72 million in cash, plus existing indebtedness.
NRG is a  manufacturer of polyisocyanurate foam insulation products used primarily in commercial and industrial roofing systems and residential buildings.
NRG is headquartered in Portland, Maine, and has annual sales of about $150 million. The privately held company has about 365 employees.
