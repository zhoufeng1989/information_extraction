A block of 60 million shares in Italian agro-industry and chemical group Montedison SpA, or around a stake of 1.52 percent, today changed hands at a price of 998 lire a share, said a note from the Milan bourse.
At 1435 GMT, Montedison shares were down 0.16 percent at 997 with a total of just over 3.1 million shares being traded today.
