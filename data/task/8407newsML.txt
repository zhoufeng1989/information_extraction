French supermarkets group Carrefour said on Thursday it had not increased its stake in its Spanish unit Pryca, denying Spanish stock market rumours to that effect.
"We deny this rumour. There has been no change in our stake," a Carrefour spokeswoman said.
Carrefour owns 68.6 percent of Pryca. Shares in the Spanish unit were up on the Madrid market.
Carrefour shares were down 19 francs at 2,581, or 0.73 percent, at 1009 GMT, while the Paris CAC 40 index of leading shares was up 0.24 percent.
