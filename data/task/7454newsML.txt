Indonesia's PT Darya-Varia Laboratoria said on Wednesday it posted a net profit of 9.32 billion rupiah in the first six months of 1996 against 8.26 billion rupiah in the same period last year.  
Six months to June 30, 1996
(in billion rupiah unless stated)
    Net sales			 92.58 vs 67.00
    Gross profit		    47.39 vs 33.80
    Operating profit		22.73 vs 15.44
    Pre-tax profit		  12.17 vs 12.41
    Net profit			 9.32 vs 8.26
    Earnings per share (rupiah)    73 vs 66
    Retained earnings	     22.89 vs 12.26
Note:The consolidated results were unaudited. PT Darya-Varia Laboratoria is a pharmaceutical and health care products manufacturer.
