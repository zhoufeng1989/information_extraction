Hong Kong-listed construction group CIG-WH International (Holdings) Ltd said on Wednesday it had agreed to place 40 million new shares, or 16.7 percent of its expanded share capital, with private investors at HK$1.25 per share.
The company's shares ended at HK$1.27 on Tuesday.
CIG-WH said in a statement that net proceeds of HK$48.5 million would be used to expand the company's existing business operations and to purchase new business ventures.
The placing is due to be completed on September 3, it said.
Kingsway SE Securities Ltd is the placing agent.
CIG-WH, which listed in December, reported a profit on Tuesday of HK$33.33 million for the year to March 31.
