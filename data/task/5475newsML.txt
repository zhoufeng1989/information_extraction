Finnish state-owned bank Postipankki Oy said in a statement on Wednesday it will issue three 200 million markka bonds with the maturities two, four and eight years.
The two-year bond will carry a 4.0 percent coupon, the four-year bond a 5.25 percent coupon and the eight-year bond a 6.25 percent coupon, Postipankki said.
The bonds will be issued at variable rates, it noted.
The subscription period will run from September 9 to November 8, 1996, it said.
