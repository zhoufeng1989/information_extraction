Spanish stainless steel firm Acerinox said its first half profit fell to 9.428 billion pesetas on group turnover of 92.038 and cash-flow of 14.146 billion pesetas.
It did not give comparative figures.
At the same time it said that, given the strength of its order book, it was raising its prices by five percent from September 1.  
Results were as follows:
 Net taxed				9.428  vs   17.984
 Turnover				92.038  vs  103.260
 Cash flow			     14.146  vs   19.859
