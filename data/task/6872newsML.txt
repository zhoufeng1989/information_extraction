McDermott International Inc said on Wednesday that Brock Hattox, its chief financial officer, is resigning from his various positions at the company.
Hattox is resigning from his positions as executive vice president, chief financial officer and as president of the engineering and construction group.
He also is stepping down from the boards of both McDermott International and J. Ray McDermott SA, a majority-owned subsidiary of the energy-services company.
His resignation is effective September 1. The company said Hattox is pursing "other interests." No successor was named.
