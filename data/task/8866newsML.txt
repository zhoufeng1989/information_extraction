Donaldson Lufkin & Jenrette Inc said it agreed to sell seven million shares of 8.42 preferred trust securities at $25 per share.
The company said the securities will be issued and proceeds received on August 27.
The securities will be issued through an affiliated trust, DLJ Capital Trust I, and will be listed on the New York Stock Exchange, DLJ said.
It said proceeds will be used for general corporate purposes.
