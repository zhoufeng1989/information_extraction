Nomura Research Institute Ltd upgraded Matsushita-Kotobuki Electronics Industries Ltd to a "1" from its previous "2", market sources said on Wednesday.
In its three-grade rating system, the research institute assigns a "1" rating to issues whose values it expects will outperform the key 225-share Nikkei average by more than 10 percentage points over the following six months.
Nomura officials were not immediately available for comment.
