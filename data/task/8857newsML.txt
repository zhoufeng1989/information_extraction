American Phoenix Group Inc and Kushi Macrobiotics Corp said they filed a joint registration statement with the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) with details of their planned merger.
The companies said they expect the merger to become effective after review by the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC).
The companies said they amended the terms of the warrants whereby the exercise price of the warrants was reduced by $2.50, from $6.25 to $3.75 per share.
