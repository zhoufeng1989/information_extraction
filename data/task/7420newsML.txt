Bottling plant operator Shibuya Kogyo Co Ltd's shares were higher by midday on Wednesday, after the company raised its earnings forecast, brokers said.
At midday, its shares were 100 yen higher at 1,700 yen. Trade volume was 61,000 shares.
On Tuesday, Shibuya Kogyo revised upward its current parent forecast to 3.0 billion yen for the year ending June 1997 from a previous forecast of 1.16 billion yen.
