The Polish construction firm Elektromontaz Export SA signed a $1.3 million contract with the Czech firm Skoda Energetika, Elektromontaz said in a statement.
Work on the project, cable installation at the Czech firm's plant in Plzen, Western Bohemia, will take place from the beginning of September to the end of June next year, the statement said.
It gave no further details.
