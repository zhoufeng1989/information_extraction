Shares in French media group Pathe firmed strongly on Wednesday, on the back of strong first-half sales and bullish sentiment on British broadcaster BSkyB Plc, in which it holds 17 percent.
Traders said NatWest upgraded Pathe to a "buy" from "hold" following the sales figures, which showed turnover of 989 million for the first half compared to proforma 440 million a year-ago.
Pathe was up 45 francs to 1,285, or 3.63 percent at 0918 GMT, while the wider market was down 0.56 percent on consolidation.
