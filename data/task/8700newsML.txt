The Washington Post Co said on Thursday it agreed to acquire TechNews Inc, publisher of Washington Technology newspaper.
The company did not disclose details of the deal.
Washington Technology, based in Vienna, Va., has a national circulation of 40,000 in the information technology service industry.
TechNews also publishes an annual Almanac, a directory of local technology companies, Washington Technology Online, and other print and electronic products.
Washington Technology founder Esther Smith will remain editor-at-large, with Tim Karney as publisher. John Sanders, current chairman of TechNews, will become a consultant to the company.
Washington Technology will establish an editorial advisory board chaired by David Ignatius, the Post's assistant managing editor for business, the company said.
