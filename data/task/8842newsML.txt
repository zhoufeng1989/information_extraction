Great Southern Bancorp Inc said Thursday its directors voted a two-for-one stock split.  
The stock split requires approval by a majority of the company's shareholders of a proposal to increase authorized shares to 20 million from 10 million. Shareholders will vote on the proposal at an October 16, 1996 annual meeting.
If approved, the stock split will be effective October 21, record October 11.
Great Southern Bancorp has about $670 million in assets.
