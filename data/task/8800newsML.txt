PAREXEL International Corp said Thursday it had acquired a health care consulting firm in a transaction designed to pool interests for financial accounting purposes.
PAREXEL said it had bought State and Federal Associates Inc in exchange for about $20 million in common stock.
The firm will help PAREXEL to provide client services like clinical research on product safety, it said.
PAREXEL is a contract research organization that provides clinical research and development to pharmaceutical, biotechnical and medical device industries.
