Shares in Dutch insurer Aegon NV were 3.10 guilders firmer at 83.40 on Thursday after the group posted higher-than-expected first half net profits of 711.1 million guilders.
Net profit rose more than 15 percent from the year-earlier period, bettering analysts' forecasts of a figure between 679 and 699 million guilders.
The insurer also upgraded its expectations for the full 1996 result, saying earnings and earnings per share would be in line with the first half's respective rises of 15.7 and 15 percent.
