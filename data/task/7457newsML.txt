Indonesia's PT Roda Vivatex said on Wednesday it posted a net profit of 13.94 billion rupiah in the first six months of 1996 against 15.44 billion rupiah in the same period last year.  
Six months to June 30, 1996
(in billion rupiah unless stated)
    Net sales				  80.02 vs 83.27
    Gross profit			     22.12 vs 26.99
    Operating profit			 17.72 vs 21.66
    Pre-tax profit			   17.86 vs 20.79
    Net profit				 13.94 vs 15.44
    Earnings per share (in rupiah)	 166 vs 184
    Retained earnings			94.58 vs 70.09
Note: The consolidated results were unaudited. PT Roda Vivatex is a textile company.
