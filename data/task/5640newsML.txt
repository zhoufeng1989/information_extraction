Hometown Bancorp Inc said Wednesday its shareholders had agreed to let HUBCO acquire it.
Hometown, parent company of The Bank of Darien, said the shareholders approved the acquisition by the Mahwah, N.J., bank at a special meeting on August 20.
The acquisition, subject to approval by the Connecticut banking department, would likely occur at the end of August, it added.
Hometown said each outstanding common share would be converted into the right to receive $17.75 in cash.
