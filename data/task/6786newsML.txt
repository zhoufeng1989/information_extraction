Qatar Airways carried 4,861 tonnes of cargo in the first seven months of this year compared with 3,572 tonnes for the whole of 1995, chief executive Sheikh Hamad bin Ali bin Jabr al-Thani said.
In its first year of operation, 1994, the airline carried 1,472 tonnes.
Sheikh Hamad said the airline expected to carry around 400,000 passengers this year. He gave no comparison.
Seven prominent Qatari businessmen are the major shareholders in Qatar Airways. It flies to 26 destinations with a fleet of two Boeing 747s and three Boeing 727s. A fourth used 727 is due to be delivered next week. --Air Cargo Newsroom Tel +44 171 542 8982 Fax +44 171 542 5017
