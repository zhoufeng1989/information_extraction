The Paris airports authority, Aeroports de Paris (ADP), said New York was the top airport gateway for imports to Charles de Gaulle and Orly Airports in the first seven months of 1996.
New York -Paris air cargo volume totalled 23,183 tonnes, down 3.0 percent on the same period last year, ADP figures showed.
In second place, was Chicago, with 16,890 tonnes, up 13.0 percent on the first seven months of 1995.
Hong Kong was ranked third with 10,159 tonnes flown to Paris last month, a fall of 11.4 percent on 1995 levels, ADP said.
