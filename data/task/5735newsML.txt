Barnett Banks Inc said on Wednesday that it set a two-for-one split of its common stock.
The split will take effect September 6, applicable to shareholders of record as of that date.
Shareholders will be paid a quarterly dividend of $0.54 per share on a pre-split basis on October 1.
The future indicated quarterly dividend rate will be $0.27 per share, the company said.
The split will leave Barnett with 192 million shares outstanding.
