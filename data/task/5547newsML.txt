Cinemark USA Inc said it completed the issuance of $200 million 9-5/8 percent senior subordinated notes due 2008.
The notes were issued at 99.553 percent of the principal face amount, the company said.
Proceeds from the issuance of the subordinated notes were $193.2 million, enabling the company to repurchase $123.4 million of its 12 percent senor notes due June 1, 2002.
The company will use remaining proceeds for general corporate purposes, including theater development and reducing borrowing through a revolving credit facility, the company said.
