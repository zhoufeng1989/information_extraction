Radnet Inc said on Wednesday that it named Don Bulens president and chief executive.
Bulens was formerly vice president of worldwide partners for Lotus Development Corp, a subsisidiary of IBM Corp.
Bulens replaces Radnet founder Ed Fredkin, who will continue as chairman.
Radnet develops and markets WebShare, designed to ease the implementation of customized groupware systems using corporate intranets and the World Wide Web.
