The volume of goods handled by Senegal's port of Dakar fell more than 13 percent in the first six months of 1996, Dakar's Chamber of Commerce and Industry said.
The Chamber said in a statement Thursday the total dropped from 3.03 million tonnes in the first six months of 1995 to 2.6 million tonnes during the same period this year.
Dakar handles more than 90 percent of Senegal's exports.
