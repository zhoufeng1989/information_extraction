Racotek Inc said Thursday it named David Maenke as vice president of finance, chief financial officer and corporate secretary, effective immediately.
Maenke previously served as vice president and chief financial officer of DataServ Inc, Racotek said.  
Maenke replaces James Flaherty, who was named to the newly created position of corporate controller, the company said.
