Amriyah Cement made a net after-tax profit of 150 million pounds in the financial year 1995/6, up from 133.755 million in the previous year, company sources said on Wednesday. The years end on June 30.
The dividend for the year will be not less than 5.00 pounds, against 4.50 for the previous year, the company said. The profits are equivalent to 7.50 pounds for each of the 20 million shares.
Further details were not immediately available.
Amriyah shares last traded on Wednesday at 53.00 pounds, up from 52.81 at the end of trading on Tuesday.
