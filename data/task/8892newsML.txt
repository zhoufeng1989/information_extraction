The Chicago Board Options Exchange (CBOE) said on Thursday it would list options on shares of Saks Holdings Inc on August 29.
The options will have initial expirations of September, October, January and April, with strike prices set at 30, 35 and 40.
Saks shares were up 1/4 at 33-5/8 in late morning trading on Thursday.
