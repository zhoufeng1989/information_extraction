Alliance Capital Management LP said on Thursday that it and Albion Asset Advisors LLC have formed Albion Alliance LLC, a company dedicated to the management of private investments on behalf of institutional and large private investors.
Alliance said that Mark Arnold and Alastair Tedford, co-founders of Albion Asset Advisors, are directing the new company.
Albion Alliance will have a global focus and will expand Alliance's existing corporate finance and private investing business, particularly into emerging markets, the company said.
