Morgan Stanley & Co International Ltd exercised an over-allotment option on Tuesday requiring Kerry Properties Ltd to issue an additional 9.68 million shares, or 1.0 percent of its expanded share capital, at HK$17.50 each, the investment bank said on Wednesday.
The share price is exclusive of brokerage fees and the stock exchange transaction levy, Morgan Stanley said.
Kerry Properties' share capital will expand to 1,009.68 million shares following the issue, it said.
Dealing in the shares is expected to start on August 23.
Kerry plans to use the net proceeds of HK$160 million to fund existing property and infrastructure development projects in Hong Kong and China, and for general working capital.
Kerry shares ended at HK$17.60 on Tuesday.
