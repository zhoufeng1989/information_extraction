George Power Co said Wednesday it has agreed to sell nine million of preferred securities at $25 per share.
Quarterly distributions on the preferred shares will be payable at a rate of 7.75 percent per year.
The closing is scheduled for August 28.
The securities will be issued through Georgia Power Capital Trust I, and the $225 million sale will be handled by an underwriting group headed by Lehman Brothers.
George Power is a subsidiary of Southern Co.
