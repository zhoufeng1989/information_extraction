Shares in National Westminster Bank Plc slipped early on Wednesday after SBC Warburg downgraded the stock to "hold' from "buy', traders said.
By 0834 GMT NatWest shares were trading 14p lower at 675p.
Warburg analysts declined to comment on the downgrade.
Elsewhere in the sector profit-taking pushed Barclays Plc shares 7p lower to 924p.
