Shares in Finnish sporting goods group Amer-yhtyma Oy on Wednesday added to Tuesday's strong gains amid sustained lively foreign demand, brokers said.
Brokers were again unable to pinpoint the factors behind the demand but one said that there had been positive analysts' comments on the company earlier in August.
At 1220/0920 GMT, Amer traded at a 23-month high 118.00 markka, up 9.10 markka or more than eight percent, after hitting 120.00 markka earlier. Trade was a lively 13 million markka.
