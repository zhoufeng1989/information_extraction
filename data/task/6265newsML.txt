City Developments Limited said on Wednesday its subsidiary Branbury Investments Ltd (Branbury) has proposed to issue Singapore $210 million secured fixed rate bonds.  
The company said in a statement Branbury intends to apply the proceeds from the bond issue to finance an acquisition of a six-storey industrial building in Singapore.
It said the bonds would be issued in denominations of S$250,000 each and would bear interest at a fixed rate of 4.875 percent per year payable semi-annually.
Citicorp Investment Bank (Singapore) Limited has been appointed the manager, arranger and underwriter for the bond issue.
