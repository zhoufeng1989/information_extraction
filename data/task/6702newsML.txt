LONDON, August 21 (LPC) - Julian Hodge Bank has signed a 51 million stg revolver, arranger Lloyds Bank Capital Markets announced.
The credit, which marks Julian Hodge Bank's debut in the syndicated loan market, will be used for general corporate purposes.
It is divided into two equal tranches, with three- and five-year maturities. The shorter tranche is priced at 35 basis points over LIBOR and the other at 40 bps over.
Subscribing banks are Lloyds Bank, BZW, Royal Bank of Scotland, Yamaichi Bank, Banca Nazionale del Lavoro and Brown, Shipley & Co.
