Metz-Nancy-Lorraine Airport said it handled 2,131 tonnes of freight and mail traffic in the first seven months of the year, a decline of 2.7 percent on January-July 1996.
The only "all-cargo" service at the airport is a nightly Mulhouse-Metz-Paris-CDG-Metz-Mulhouse rotation operated by French airline, L'Aeropostale, using Boeing 737 QC (quick change) aircraft.
The service carries mail and express shipments.
French airline, TAT European Airlines, owned by British Airways PLC operates a daily Metz to Paris-Orly passenger service using Fokker 28 aircraft.
