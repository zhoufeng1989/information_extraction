Peoples Heritage Financial Group Inc's acquisition of Family Bancorp has been approved by shareholders of both companies, Peoples Heritage said Thursday.
Their agreement calls for Family Bancorp shareholders to receive 1.26 shares of Peoples Heritage common stock for each share of Family common stock.
Family Bancorp shares were up 3/8 at 26-3/8 on Thursday afternoon, while Peoples Heritage was unchanged at 21-3/8.
Peoples Heritage is a $4.4 billion banking and financial services holding company. Family Bancorp is an $892 million holding company for Family Bank FSB.
