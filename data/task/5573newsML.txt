John Deere Capital Corp, a unit of Deere & Co, filed Wednesday a shelf registration with the Securities and Exchange Commission for up to $1.0 billion in debt securities and warrants to buy debt securities.
The Deere unit said that when combined with previous registrations, it will have up to $1,208,850,000 in debt securities available for sale.
It said net proceeds will be used for working capital and other general corporate purposes.
The offering will be made through Merrill Lynch & Co, Goldman Sachs & Co and Salomon Bros Inc.
John Deer Capital provides financing for retail purchases of new and used John Deere agricultural, industrial, commercial and consumer equipment.
