Leading Lithuanian commercial bank Vilnius Bank on Thursday said it made net income in the first half of the year of 11.69 million litas, a 32 percent rise on first quarter earnings.
Vilnius, in which Bankers Trust has a 50 percent stake, said its first half net interest income was 17.11 million litas, service charges and other income 14.06 million litas and foreign exchange gains 6.17 million litas.
Total assets were 483.84 million litas, up from 382.91 million litas at the end of 1995.
