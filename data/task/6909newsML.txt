Stratosphere Corp said Wednesday it hired Donaldson, Lufkin & Jenrette to advise it on alternatives to maximize shareholder value.
Possible alternatives include a debt restructuring and infusion of additional capital through a rights offering, the casino company said.
The company's statement did not give further details on its plans.
Stratosphere is 42 percent owned by Grand Casinos Inc.
