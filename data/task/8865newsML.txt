TPI Enterprises Inc said Thursday that its shareholders had approved a reorganization plan under which Shoney's Inc will acquire nearly all of TPI's assets.
TPI, a restaurant franchisee, is to be acquired in exchange for 5,577,102 shares of Shoney's plus an additional $10 million of Shoney's stock, based on the average closing market price for the 10 trading days prior to the closing, the companies said previously.
The transaction is expected to be closed on September 9.
