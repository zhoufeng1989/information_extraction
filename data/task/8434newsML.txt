The price of BASF AG shares improved on Thursday due to its better than expected half year results. At 0900 GMT BASF was up 51 pfennigs at 42.75 marks.
On Thursday morning BASF published its half-year results as the last of the Germany's three chemical giants. It posted a pretax profit of 2.32 billion marks up from 2.03 billion the year before.
"That was just a bit better than expected," a trader commented. At 0900 GMT the 30-share DAX was down 4.74 points at 2,539.00.
