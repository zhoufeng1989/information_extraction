BTR Plc, the diversified industrial group, has refused to comment on a press report that it is close to clinching the sale of its Tilcon business.
A BTR spokesman said that Tilcon was one of a series of companies rumoured to be on the sale-list as the group seeks to rationalise its operations and focus on core businesses. But he delcined to comment on a report that CRH Plc, the Irish building materials group was close to agreement to buy Tilcon, a concrete and stone business, for 180 million stg.  
The report in the Evening Standard newspaper said that Tilcon would be sold for 180 million, a price that beat off other potential buyers.
BTR says it will give an update on the rationalisation progress when it announces its results on September 12.
