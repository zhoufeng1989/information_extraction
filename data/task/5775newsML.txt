BTR Plc is in discussions about selling Tilcon, its U.S. roadstone business, to CRH Plc, the Financial Times reported.
The sale talks involve a price of about 180 million stg for Tilcon, the newspaper said.
The paper said neither BTR or CRH would comment, but said it is understood that CRH directos visiting subsidiaries in the northeast U.S. will meet with BTR chief executive Ian Strachan.
