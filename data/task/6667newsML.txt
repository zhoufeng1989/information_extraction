Shares in J. Sainsbury Plc slipped on Wednesday amid talk that NatWest Securities had cut profit forecasts for the supermarket chain, market sources said.
Pre-tax profit forecasts had been cut to 750 million stg from 760 million stg for fiscal 1997, said one.
Sainsbury shares eased 9p to 398 on trading volume of 6.5 million shares.
NatWest last week issued a "trading sell" recommendation on the stock.
NatWest analysts were not immediately available to comment.
