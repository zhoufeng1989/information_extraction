CCF Holding Co said Thursday it planned to repurchase up to 10 percent of its 1.0 million outstanding common shares on the open market.  
CCF, the holding company of Clayton County Federal Savings and Loan Association, said it was waiting to get approval before buying up to 1,101,766 shares.
The company's board authorized it to repurchase shares on July 3.
