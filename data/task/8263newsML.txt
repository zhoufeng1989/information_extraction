Eagle Hardware & Garden Inc said Thursday it had filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission for an offering of 4.5 million shares.
An additional 675,000 shares would cover over-allotments, the company said.
Proceeds from the sale will be used to finance new store openings in existing markets and expansion into new markets, to repay short-term bank borrowings, and for other general corporate purposes, Eagle Hardware said.  
The offering will be managed by Montgomery Securities and Dain Bosworth Inc.
