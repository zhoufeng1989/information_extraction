China has found a natural gas field with estimated deposits of 20 billion tonnes in the Junggar Basin of the northwestern region of Xinjiang, the Xinhua news agency said on Wednesday.
The field was discovered in Hutubi county on August 6 and extended over 23 square km (8.9 square miles), Xinhua said.
Xinhua said initial testing at one well indicated it could be produce 800,000 cubic metres of natural gas a day, it said.
