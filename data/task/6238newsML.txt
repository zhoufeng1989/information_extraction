Singapore's Urban Redevelopment Authority said on Wednesday City Developments Ltd had tendered the highest bid of Singapore $118.80 million for a residential site in a government land sale.
The price for the Trevose Cresent site worked out at S$5,379.22 per sq metre, it said.
Another residential site at Binjai View attracted a highest tendered price of S$40.28 million from Allgreen Properties Ltd and Hoe Seng Company Pte Ltd.
Property analysts said the prices were slightly lower than market expectations. -- Singapore newsroom 65-8703080
