-- Hambrecht & Quist said it raised its earnings estimates for Pediatrix Medical Group Inc after the company's announcement that it acquired a seven-physician neonatal practice in West Palm Beach, Florida.
-- The investment firm raised 1996 earnings estimate to $0.89 a share from $0.87 and its 1997 estimate to $1.23 from $1.14.
-- Hambrecht & Quist repeated its buy rating on the stock.
-- Pediatrix shares closed up 1-1/4 at 46-3/4 on Wednesday.
