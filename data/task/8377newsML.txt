Iberia's passenger load factor rose to 73.08 percent in July from 70.46 percent in June, the airline said.
It carried 1.36 million passengers in the month, taking the total so far this year to 8.31 million, and flew 2.55 billion revenue passenger kms (14.38 billion January to end July).
Freight traffic rose seven percent in the month to 59.73 million freight tonne kms, taking the accumulated total to 416.28 tonne kms, 10.1 percent more than in the same period last year.
