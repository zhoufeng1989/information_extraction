DataWorks Corp said Wednesday it signed a definitive agreement to acquire all of the outstanding stock of DCD Corp.
DataWorks said it expected the acquisition to be completed in the fourth quarter.
DataWorks plans to issue up to 1.8 million shares of its common stock to pay for the acquisition.
The stock rose 3-1/2 to a year-high of 22-3/4 on Wednesday.
DCD, based in Minneapolis, develops business management software for the make-to-order manufacturing segment.
DataWorks, based in San Diego, is a supplier of software for discrete and repetitive midrange manufacturers.
