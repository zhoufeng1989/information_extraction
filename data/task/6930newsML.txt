Cylink Corp said on Wednesday that it won a three-year contract to supply security systems to Concert, a joint venture between British Telecommunications Plc and MCI Communications Corp.
Concert, based in Reston, Virginia, provides managed network services on a global basis.
Cylink said its technology, including its SecureFrame, SecureDomain, and SecureManager products, will be used with the Concert Frame Relay Service for customers of all sizes.
