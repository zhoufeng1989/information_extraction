SatCon Technology Corp said Thursday its chief executive, David Eisenhaure, and its financial chief, Michael Turmelle, recently bought company stock on the open market.
Turmelle bought 1,300 shares and Eisenhaure bought 4,000, the company said. A company spokesman declined to disclose the purchase prices.
In late morning trading, SatCon was up 3/8 at 8-7/8 in thin volume.
