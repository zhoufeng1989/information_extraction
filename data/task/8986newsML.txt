LONDON, Aug 22 (LPC) - Skanska Capital AB has increased the size of its new revolving credit to $700 million, according to arrangers Chase Investment bank and Deutsche Morgan Grenfell.
The deal, which refinances existing debt as well as providing new money, was launched at $650 million but upped after banks offered commitments of more than $800 million. The deal is guaranteed by Skanska AB.
Signing is slated for Tuesday, September 3 in Malmo, Sweden. The credit has a seven-year tenor and is priced 18 bps over LIBOR with a commitment fee of 8.5 bps.
