The Warsaw Bourse's board cleared shares of  Bank Komunalny SA w Gdyni for trade on the main market, it said in a statement.
The bourse also cleared 1.25 million D series shares of PPABank SA for trade on the main floor.
It said, however, that confectioner Jutrzenka SA did not fulfil requirements to move from the parallel market to the main floor.
