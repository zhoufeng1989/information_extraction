Superconductor Technologies Inc said Wednesday it has filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission for a public offering of two million shares of common stock.
Underwriters have been granted an option to purchase 300,000 shares of stock to cover over-allotments.
Proceeds will be used to purchase capital equipment, fund product development and for working capital and other general corporate purposes.
Managing underwriters for the offering are Rauscher Pierce Refsnes Inc, Van Kasper & Co and H.C. Wainwright Co Inc.
