MEDIQ Inc said Thursday its subsidiary MEDIQ/PRN Life Support Services Inc had begun a cash tender offer to buy all of PRN's senior secured notes at $1,078.74 per $1,000 principal amount.
The company said the 12 1/8 percent notes were due August 1999 and their outstanding principal balance was $100 million.
The offer to expires September 23, it said.
MEDIQ said the offer was to reduce the company's interest expense by replacing the notes with lower interest bearing loans under a credit facility.
