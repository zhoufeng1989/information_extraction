The Hong Kong Economic Journal said on Wednesday the government had set a land premium of HK$2.6 billion for a site in Tseung Kwan O to be jointly developed by Sun Hung Kai Properties Ltd and Hong Kong Oxygen & Acetylene Co Ltd.
The figure represents a an average land premium of HK$1,800 per sq ft of gross floor area, the Chinese language daily said.
The 182,480-sq-ft site in the New Territories is expected to generate a gross floor area of 1.45 million sq ft.
Executives from Sun Hung Kai, Hong Kong Oxygen and the government were not immediately available for comment.
