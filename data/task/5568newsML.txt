PaineWebber Inc's Private Client Group said on Wednesday it added Lowe's Cos to its 30-stock focus list.
The focus list consists of stocks rated buy or attractive by the firm's research department, PaineWebber said.
Lowe's, a home improvement retailer, reported 1996 revenues of $7.08 billion and earnings of $1.41 per share. For 1997, PaineWebber estimates earnings at $1.65 per share.
