* MacNeal-Schwendler Corp reported second quarter earnings of $0.14 per share, down from $0.26 last year. Shares closed at 7-3/8.  
* Mac Frugal's Bargains-Closeouts Inc reported second quarter earnings of $0.12 per share, compared to $0.13 last year. Shares closed at 19-3/8.
* Emerson Radio Corp reported a second quarter loss of $0.12 per share, compared to a loss of $0.04 last year. Shares closed at 2-5/8.
