Airport Systems International Inc said Thursday it had been awarded contracts totaling $1.2 million to supply ground-based navigation equipment for Beirut International Airport and airfield signage for the airport serving Fairbanks, Alaska.
At July 31 the company's backlog stood at $4.5 million, up from $4.3 million a year ago, it said.
Airport Systems, reporting higher results for its fiscal first quarter ended July 31, said cash flow from operations amounted to $2.4 million for the quarter. At the end of the quarter it had $3 million in cash and cash equivalents, it said.
