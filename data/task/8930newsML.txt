Coca-Cola Enterprises said Thursday it has signed letters of intent to acquire Coke West and a related company in a deal worth an estimated $158 million.
Coca-Cola Enterprises will be acquiring the outstanding shares of Coke West and Grand Forks Coca-Cola Bottling Co, as well as the convertible debt and warrants held by The Coca-Cola Co.
Coca-Cola Enterprises said it expects to finance the deal by issuing debt. The transaction is expected to be closed by the end of this year's third quarter.
