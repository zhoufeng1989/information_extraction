The weighted average auction price of Takefuji Corp shares, due to go public on the over-the-counter market on August 30, is 9,691 yen, the Japan Securities Dealers' Association (JSDA) said on Wednesday.
Japan's largest consumer finance company is offering five million shares to the public, half via an auction.
JSDA said there were a total 8.24 million bids, with the lowest accepted bid at 9,100 yen and the highest at 15,700 yen.
Market sources said the lowest bid for the share was 5,660 yen.
