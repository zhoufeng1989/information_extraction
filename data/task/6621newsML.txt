Blue chip Pakistan State Oil rose 7.0 rupees, or 2.15 percent to 332 on news that the government increased the price on various petroleum products by 1.5 percent to 6.75 percent, dealers said.
A turnover of 9,300 shares of the stock was recorded by midsession.
"The increase is a result of price movements in the international market. We believe that the price increases will marginally increase the profitability of the sector," a dealer at Barings Securities said. -- Karachi newsroom 9221-5685192
