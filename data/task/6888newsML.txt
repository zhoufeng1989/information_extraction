American Woodmark Corp said on Wednesday that its president, Jake Gosa, would assume the additional post of chief executive officer.
William Brandt Jr, who formerly held the post, will continue as chairman, the company said.
Gosa was also chief operating officer before his appointment as CEO.
Representatives of the company were not available for comment, and a new COO was not named in the announcement.
