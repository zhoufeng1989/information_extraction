Rymer Foods Inc said Wednesday it adjourned a special shareholders meeting called to approve a proposed sale of certain of the company's assets.
The meeting was adjourned to Wednesday, August 28, 1996.  
The company said that solicitation of proxies will continue until August 28.
In January, Rymer agreed to sell the assets of its seafood brokerage business for about $12.7 million to a company formed by the president of the operation. The proposed transaction was subject to approval of 66-2/3 percent of Rymer's outstanding common stock and holders of a majority of Rymer's outstanding 11 percent senior notes.
Rymer supplies portion-controlled meat and seafood products to foodservice and retail markets.
