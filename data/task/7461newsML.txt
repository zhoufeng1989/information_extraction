Indonesia's PT Tigaraksa Satria said on Wednesday it posted a net profit of 13.62 billion rupiah inn the first six months of 1996 against 12.26 billion rupiah in the same period last year.  
Six months to June 30, 1996
(in billion rupiah unless stated)
    Net sales			     246.08 vs 253.87
    Gross profit			   84.16 vs 77.14
    Operating profit		     31.38 vs 30.40
    Pre-tax profit			 25.57 vs 26.73
    Profit before minority interest  17.56 vs 16.73
    Net profit			     13.62 vs 12.26
    Retained earnings		    65.97 vs 48.22
    Earnings per share (in rupiah)     277 vs 303
Note: The consolidated results were unaudited. PT Tigaraksa Satria is engaged in the distribution of consumer goods and has an interest in property.
