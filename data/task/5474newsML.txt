State Commercial Bank of Greece dismissed press reports regarding the partial sale of its subsidiary Bank of Attica and underlined that negotiations with the interested parties were continuing.
"As regards several press reports on the sale by Commercial Bank of a stake in Bank of Attica, it is clarified that the pertinent negotiations are still going on and have not been finalised," the bank said in a statement.
