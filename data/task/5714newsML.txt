Ultramar Corp said on Wednesday that Jack Drosdick will step down as president and chief operating officer on August 31, to pursue other business and personal interests.
The company said it will consolidate its leadership and corporate structure, with four business units now reporting to Jean Gaulin, Ultramar's chairman and chief executive.
The position of president and chief operating officer will be eliminated and Jean Gaulin will assume the additional responsibilities, the petroleum refining and marketing company said.
