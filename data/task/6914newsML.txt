Cannon Express Inc said on Wednesday that it expects revenues for its fiscal year ending June 30, 1997 to be over $100 million.
Cannon Express reported earning of $0.67 a share on revenues of $89.99 million in fiscal 1996, compared to $1.84 per share on revenues of $79.0 illion the year before.
The company aid that it is encouraged by recent increased demand for the company's truckload transportation services.
Cannon said that it believes that continued increased demand could have a positive impact on fiscal 1997 revenue, profitability and earnings.
The company said that the cost of fuel is currently above historic levels, although down slightly from earlier in the 1996 calendar year.
