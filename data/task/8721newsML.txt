Crown Cork & Seal Co Inc said it was comfortable with the lower-end of analysts' 1996 earnings per share estimates.
According to First Call, analysts expect the company to earn between $2.15 to $2.75 a share for 1996, with a mean of $2.35 a share. Crown Cork earned $0.83 a share for the 1995 full year. The company also said it was comfortable with the middle range of earnings estimates for 1997. Analysts estimates range from $3.00 to $3.75 a share, with a mean of $3.37, according to First Call.
