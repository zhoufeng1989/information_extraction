Shares in Smithkline Beecham Plc climbed on Wednesday as bid talk continued to surround the pharmaceutical sector, traders said.
"We have heard very, very vague rumours -- we can't actually pinpoint where they're coming from -- that supposedly Roche are looking to make a bid for the company," said one.
"To be honest we don't really believe it," he added.
One trader said the speculation had originated in the U.S..
Shares in Zeneca were also ahead on similar rumours of possible interest from Swiss company Roche Holding AG.
