The French Associes en Finance research institute said their August poll of 18 brokerages showed that French corporate forecasts had again been reduced for both 1996 and 1997.
The 1996 forecasts, on an earnings per share basis, were downgraded by an average 1.8 percent and those for 1997 by 0.8 percent compared to the July poll.
For the CAC-40 companies the decline was less pronounced at 0.4 percent for both 1996 and 1997.
