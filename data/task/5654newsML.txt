Federal Reserve Bank of Kansas City said Wednesday Herman Cain, chairman of the board of directors of the bank, has resigned as chairman and director.  
The Fed statement said Cain plans to become actively involved in the national political campaign.
Cain, chairman and chief executive officer of Godfather's Pizza based in Omaha, Neb., has been a strong supporter of the Republican party.
In announcing Cain's resignation, Federal Reserve Bank president Thomas Hoenig said, "Herman is an outstanding individual and his leadership will be missed by our board."
