KinderCare Learning Centers Inc said on Thursday it posted increases in EBITDA as well as revenues of $541.3 million for fiscal 1996.
EBITDA, for the fourth quarter of fiscal 1996, including non-recurring items recorded in both fiscal years, increased 0.8 percent or $0.2 million to $24.4 million, the company said.
Excluding non-recurring items recorded in both fiscal years, EBITDA for the fourth quarter fiscal 1996 increased 15.8 percent or $3.7 million to $26.9 million from $23.2 million, and as a percentage of revenues, increased 1.4 percent to 20.3 percent from 18.9 percent, the company said.
