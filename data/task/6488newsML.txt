The Federal Home Loan Mortgage Corp (Freddie Mac or FHLMC) said it issued Real Estate Mortgage Investment Conduit (REMIC) securities totaling $300 million.
REMIC series 1885 has a coupon of 8.5 percent and is backed by $150 million of 30-year single-family fixed-rate mortgages, Freddie Mac said. Underwritten by Salomon Inc, the issue priced August 14 and has a settlement date of September 30, the agency said.
REMIC series 1887 has a coupon of 7.5 percent and is backed by $150 million of 15-year single-family fixed-rate mortgages, FHLMC said. Undewritten by Nomura Securities Co Ltd, the issue priced August 16 and has a settlement date of September 30, Freddie Mac said.
