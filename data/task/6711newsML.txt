Lagoven, a subsidiary of Petroleos de Venezuela S.A. (PDVSA), said its bunker sales during the first six months of 1996 averaged 16,000 barrels per day (bpd), double the amount it sold for the same period last year, the OPEC News Agency reported.
In a dispatch from Caracas, monitored in London, OPECNA quoted Lagoven as saying its January-June bunker sales generated revenues of some $48.9 million at an average price of $16.78 a barrel.
During the first half of 1995, bunker sales generated $23.4 million in revenue based on a price of $16.07.
