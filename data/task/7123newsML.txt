German freight forwarder Militzer & Muench GmbH (M&M) said it has expanded its air cargo activities in eastern Europe.
The M&M subsidiary in Belarus is the first forwarder in the former Soviet republic to gain the status of an International Air Transport Association (IATA) approved airfreight agency, a company statement said.
In Bulgaria, M&M is the first foreign company to become an IATA airfreight agency, the statement added.
Militzer & Muench said it has also opened a representation office in the Bulgarian industrial city of Plovdiv.
