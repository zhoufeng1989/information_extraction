Identix Inc said on Thursday that its Fingerscan Pty Ltd subsidiary in Australia had delivered its first 100 Portable Fingerscan plug-in reader versions of the company's TouchLock II fingerprint verifications system.
The delivery was made to Melbourne-based Armaguard, a division of Mayne Nickless Ltd, a cash transportation company.
The devices will be used to control access to teller machines and other cash repositories.
