MTS Systems Corp said Wednesday it reached an agreement in principle to acquire Bregenhorn-Butow & Co of Freiburg, Germany.
Financial terms of the agreement were not disclosed. The acquisition is expected to be completed during the fourth quarter of 1996, MTS said.
Bregenhorn-Butow supplies low-powered motors and drives to the European manufacturing market.
MTS manufactures computer-based testing and simulation systems.
