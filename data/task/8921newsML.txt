PepsiCo Inc's Frito-Lay Co said Thursday it named William McLaughlin as president of Frito-Lay/Europe, a unit with nearly $3 billion in retail sales in 1995.
McLaughlin succeeds Peter Thompson, who was named president and chief operating officer of Pepsi-Cola Co-International, the company said.
McLaughlin was most recently executive vice president at Frito-Lay and has served in a variety of executive posts at PepsiCo, the company said.
