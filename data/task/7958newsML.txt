Cray Electronics Plc said on Thursday it had completed the sale of its interest in P-E International.
Cray bought P-E for 17 million stg in 1993 and sold part of the operation for 4.5 million stg cash. It has now disposed of the remaining management consultancy business to Lorien Plc for 11 million cash.
