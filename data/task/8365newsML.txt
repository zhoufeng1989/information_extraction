Swedish telecoms group LM Ericsson AB said on Thursday that it had won a contract worth 400 million crowns from TT&T in Thailand to supply AXE digital telephone exchange equipment.  
The company said the AXE contract is one part of TT&T's project to improve telephone services outside Bangkok.
TT&T is a private operator that runs a local public network under a licence from the Telephone Organisation of Thailand.
