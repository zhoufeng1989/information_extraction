LONDON, Aug 22 (LPC) - Union Bank of Switzerland and Dresdner Bank said they have been asked to arrange a $20 million loan for Russia's Industry and Construction Bank.
The loan will be a short-term revolving credit with a six- month maturity and the option of two further six-month extensions. Interest is set at 550 bps over LIBOR. The deal will enter syndication in September.
Industry and Construction Bank is one of the largest banks in St. Petersburg.
