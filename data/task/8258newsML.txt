Whittman-Hart Inc's two million share public offering was priced at $28.50 per common shares, said the lead underwriter of the offering Donaldson, Lufkin & Jenrette Securities Corp.
The shares were increased to 2.0 million from 1.8 million.
The company said previously that net proceeds of the offering are expected to be used for general corporate purposes, including working capital, branch expansion and possible acquisitions of related businesses.  
Whittman-Hart is a Chicago-based provider of strategic information technology services designed to improve it clients' productivity and competitive position.
