Continental Airlines said Wednesday it had launched a service on the Internet offering travelers information on fare discounts and other promotions.
Continental said the electronic mail service would give subscribers weekly updates on the special deals.
It said it would not charge a user fee for the service, called "Continental On Line Travel Specials".
Continental said customers could subscribe to the service through the table of contents at its web site www.flycontinental.com or in the third quadrant of its web site entitled "Flying the Net".
