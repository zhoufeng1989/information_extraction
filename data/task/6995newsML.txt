The Government National Mortgage Association (GNMA or Ginnie Mae) said it increased to $200 million a previous issue of Real Estate Mortgage Investment Conduit (REMIC) securities.
REMIC 1996-19 is backed about $200 million of 7.5 percent GNMA II certificates, Ginnie Mae said.
Sponsored by Merrill Lynch & Co Inc and co-sponsored by Utendahl Capital Partners LP, the REMIC priced August 8, was upsized August 19 and has a settlement date of September 27, Ginnie Mae said.
